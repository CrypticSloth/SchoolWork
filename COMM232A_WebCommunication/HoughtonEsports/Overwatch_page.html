What is Overwatch?

Overwatch is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter. Overwatch assigns players into two teams of six, with each player selecting from a roster of 30 characters, known as "heroes", each with a unique style of play whose roles are divided into three general categories that fit their role. Players on a team work together to secure and defend control points on a map or escort a payload across the map in a limited amount of time.

Season Format

The 12 teams in our league play against each other during the regular season in best of 5 matchups. In other words, the first team to win 3 maps is the victor. Teams will play against each other and at the end of the season, the top 4 teams with the best record will go on to play in the playoffs, which will be a double elimination bracket hosted over a week at the victor schools stadium. These games are also best of 5, while the finals are a best of 7.
