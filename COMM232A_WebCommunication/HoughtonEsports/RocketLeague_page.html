What is Rocket League?

Rocket League is best described as soccer with cars. Rocket League is most commonly played in teams of 3, where each player drives a virtual car, and the goal of the game is to score the ball into the opponents goal while defending your own. The team who scores the most goals before the game ends wins. These cars however are somewhat special, as they can jump, roll, and sometimes even fly in the air. It is an extremely fast paced game that requires quick reflexes and even quicker decision making.

Season Format

The 12 teams in our league play against each other during the regular season in best of 5 matchups. In other words, the first team to win 3 matches is the victor. Teams will play against each other and at the end of the season, the top 4 teams with the best record will go on to play in the playoffs, which will be a double elimination bracket hosted over one week at the victor schools stadium. These games are also best of 5, while the finals are a best of 7.
