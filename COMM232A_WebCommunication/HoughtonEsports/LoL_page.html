What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a 5 v 5 MOBA, or Multiplayer Online Battle Arena. The goal of the game is to destroy the other the other team's 'Nexus', which is guarded by towers and the enemy team. To achieve this objective, players choose from over 100 different champions, each with their own unique abilities. Players use these champions to then work together to destroy the enemy's 'Nexus'.

Season Format

The 12 teams in our league play against each other during the regular season in best of 3 matchups. Teams will play against each other and at the end of the season. The top 4 teams with the best record will go on to play in the playoffs which will be a double elimination bracket hosted over one week at the victor school's stadium. These games are also best of 3, while the finals are a best of 5.
