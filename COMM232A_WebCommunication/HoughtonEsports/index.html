What are esports?

Esports are organized, multiplayer video game competitions particularly between professional or collegiate players. It has gained extreme popularity over the past few years and has attracted the attention of many, mainly with the large prize pools of tournemnts, one of which has approached the size of almost $30 million. It is also becoming very popular as a collegiate sport as well, attracting many students who are often offered scholarships to compete at larger schools.

About esports at Houghton

At Houghton, we compete against different schools in our regions and host multiple tournaments in our world class esports center. Come see what all of the hype is about. We have teams in Overwatch, League of Legends, Dota 2, and Rocket League. Houghton esports athletes are expected to maintain peak grades, have a regular workout regime, along with practicing with their teams
