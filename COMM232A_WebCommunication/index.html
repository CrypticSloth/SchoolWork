About Erik Sorensen

Hi! My name is Erik Sorensen and I am passionate about Programming, the Internet of Things, and Data Science, especially how machine learning can impact decision making and improve peoples lives. I aspire to analyze, visualize, and explore data to discover valuable insights and to share these insights to the world via interactive web applications. I believe that everybody can become a data scientist with the right tools. My goal is to enable people with these tools so that they can learn to scrape knowledge out of their own data, wihtout having to give their data to a 3rd party to do the work for them. My favorite quote drives this desire to deliver these apps to the world:

"An investment in knowledge pays the best interest."
-Benjamin Franklin
